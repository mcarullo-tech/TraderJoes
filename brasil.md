**Phase 1: The Rio & Football Soul (Mar 4 – Mar 8)**
- Mar 4 (Wed): Arrive Rio (GIG) 12 PM. Uber to Discovery Hostel. First night in Lapa/Glória.
- Mar 5 (Thu): Morning: Christ the Redeemer. Sunset: Arpoador Rock (Ipanema).
- Mar 6 (Fri): Morning: Hike Dois Irmãos (Vidigal). Night: Street Samba in Lapa.
- Mar 7 (Sat): Chill day. Copacabana beach and Sugarloaf Mountain.
- Mar 8 (Sun): THE BIG ONE. Carioca Final @ Maracanã (4 PM). 11:30 PM Night Bus to Ouro Preto.

**Phase 2: The Minas Mountains & The Wolf (Mar 9 – Mar 15)**
- Mar 9 (Mon): Arrive Ouro Preto (6 AM). Explore colonial hills and student bars.
- Mar 10 (Tue): Visit Mina da Passagem (Gold Mine) or hike nearby trails.
- Mar 11 (Wed): Morning bus to Santa Bárbara + taxi to Caraça Sanctuary. Wolf Night #1.
- Mar 12 (Thu): Hike to Cascatinha waterfall. Chill in the monastery. Wolf Night #2.
- Mar 13 (Fri): Taxi to train station. 1:30 PM Train to Belo Horizonte. Night in Santa Tereza.
- Mar 14 (Sat): Morning: Mercado Central (Food). Night: Authentic Samba (Bar do Orlando).
- Mar 15 (Sun): Uber to CNF airport. 2 PM Flight to Porto Seguro.

**Phase 3: The Bahia Coast (Mar 15 – Mar 23)**
- Mar 15 (Sun): Land in Porto Seguro (3 PM). Pre-booked 4x4 to Caraíva.
- Mar 16-18: Caraíva Days. River swims, Praia do Satú hike, boat to Corumbau, and nightly Forró.
- Mar 19 (Thu): Travel day. Bus from Porto Seguro to Itacaré (approx. 6-8 hours).
- Mar 20-22: Itacaré Days. Trilha das 4 Praias (Hike), surf lessons, and waterfall treks.
- Mar 23 (Mon): Morning bus to Bom Despacho + Ferry Boat into Salvador.

**Phase 4: The Salvador Finale (Mar 23 – Mar 27)**
- Mar 23 (Mon): Check into Santo Antônio Além do Carmo. Dinner with a view.
- Mar 24 (Tue): THE GOAL. Tuesday Blessing in Pelourinho. Olodum drums start around 6 PM.
- Mar 25 (Wed): Culture day. Church of Bonfim (get your ribbons) and the lower city.
- Mar 26 (Thu): Final beach day or market shopping at Mercado Modelo. Moqueca dinner.
- Mar 27 (Fri): Morning coffee in the Pelourinho. 3 PM Flight out of Salvador.
